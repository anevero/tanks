Textures

Wall
Main type of coating. A continuous block through which it is impossible to pass.

Ground
Main type of coating. Tank and robots have the fastest speed there.

Quicksand
One of the types of coating. Slows objects down.

Water
One of the types of coating. Slows objects down more than quicksand.

Forest
One of the types of coating. Slows objects down very much. Hides player's tank from robots, even if they collide in the forest.

Tanks and robots

Player's tank, which is controlled by you. This is the most powerful tank on the map: it has a lot of health and a possibility to change types of rockets.

Pink tank or simple bot
The first and the simplest modification of a robot. Shoots player's tank only if sees it in front of itself.

Green tank or improved bot
The second modification of a robot. It turns and shots player tank if it stands on the same line.

Blue tank or clever bot
The third modification of a robot. Looks for the player's tank on the map, builds the shortest way and follows it. When the player's tank is in the forest behaves like simple robot.

Obstacles and bonuses

Rocket
Tanks can launch rockets, which interacting with other objects. Rockets reduce tank's health and destroy logs, medical kits, charges.

Boom
When robots die, they explode and release an explosion, which reduces objects's health and destroys logs, medical kits, charges.

Medical kit
Periodically appears on the map and disappears after a while. Increases tanks' health, but no more than initial health. Destroyed when meets up a rocket.

Charge
Periodically appears on the map and disappears after a while. Adds rockets to random type of charge.

Log
Obstacle which you can break down or destroy by explosion or rocket. If the tank runs into an obstacle, it slows down.

Portal
Portals on the map are paired. Player's tank and robots (but not rockets) can teleport from one cell, connected with one of two related portals, to another cell, where the second portal is situated.
