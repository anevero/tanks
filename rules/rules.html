Textures

Wall
Main type of coating. The continuous block through which it is impossible to pass.

Ground
Main type of coating. Tank and robots have the fastest speed there.

Quicksand
One of the types of coating. Slows down objects.

Water
One of the types of coating. Slows down objects more than quicksand.

Forest
One of the types of coating. Slows down objects very much. Hides player's tank from robots, even if they are collided in the forest.

Tanks and robots

Yellow tank
Player's tank, which is controlled by you. This is the most powerful tank on the map: it has a lot of health and the possibility to change types of rockets.

Pink tank or simple bot
The first and the simplest modification of the robot. Shooting player's tank only if sees it in front of itself.

Green tank or improved bot
The second modification of the robot. If the player's tank stands on the same line with it, then robot turn and shoots in it.

Blue tank or clever bot
The third modification of the robot. Looks for the player's tank on the map, builds the shortest way and moves to it. When the player's tank is in the forest behaves like simple robot.

Obstacles and bonuses

Rocket
Tanks can launch rockets into other objects. Rockets take away tank's health and destroys logs, medical kits, charges.

Boom
TBD

Medical kit
Adds health to tanks and robots, but no more than initial health. Destroyed when meets up a rocket.

Charge
TBD

Log
Obstacle which you can break down or destroy by explosion or rocket. If the tank runs into an obstacle, it slows down.

Portal
Portals on the map are paired. Player's tank and robots (but not rockets) can teleport from one cell, connected with one of two related portals, to another cell, where the second portal is situated.
